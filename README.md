## About

I engineer systems that sit at the intersection of **AI research and production reality** — the layer most engineers never reach because the problems are too hard, the domains too deep, or the ambition too high.

My work spans **3D spatial computing**, **real-time computation engines**, **algorithmic finance**, and **scientific simulation**. I don't specialize in one layer of the stack. I own the full pipeline: from neural model architecture to polished frontend, from domain mathematics to deployable software.

**TypeScript** is my instrument for precision frontend and systems design. **Python** is my weapon for machine learning, computer vision, and scientific computing. Every project in this profile is evidence of what happens when deep technical knowledge meets relentless execution.

## Selected Work

<details open>
<summary><h3>🧠 Neural Holography Engine &nbsp;·&nbsp; <code>Python</code> <code>JS</code> <code>NeRF</code> <code>Three.js</code> <code>OpenCV</code></h3></summary>

> *From a flat image to a gesture-controlled hologram — end to end.*

The most ambitious project in my portfolio and one of the most technically complex systems an independent engineer can attempt. A unified AI pipeline that transforms any 2D image into a real-time, interactive 3D hologram — spanning six independent engineering layers:

| Layer | Technology | Metric |
|---|---|---|
| Neural 3D Reconstruction | TripoSR / GET3D / NeRF / CRM | 94–98% accuracy by domain |
| Mesh Optimization | Blender API / MeshLab | Sub-2s refinement |
| Real-Time Rendering | Three.js / Babylon.js | Live holographic display |
| Gesture Recognition | MediaPipe / Leap Motion | <15ms latency |
| Physical Projection | Pepper's Ghost / AR Glass | Full spatial output |

Research grounded in work from **Stability AI, NVIDIA, Google DeepMind, MIT, UC Berkeley, and ETH Zurich** (2020–2025).

**[→ Repository](https://github.com/gouravgangwardev/Neural-Holography-Engine)**

<details open>
<summary><h3>📐 Math Supernova Lab &nbsp;·&nbsp; <code>TypeScript</code> <code>Vite</code> <code>Bun</code> <code>Canvas API</code> <code>Tailwind</code></h3></summary>

> *Real-time mathematical computation, rendered at full browser speed.*

A modular, high-performance mathematics engine that parses, evaluates, and visualizes complex mathematical functions live in the browser — with zero-lag canvas rendering and instant graph updates.

Built around a clean separation of concerns: each mathematical domain is its own isolated module. Memoization prevents redundant computation. Lazy loading keeps the initial bundle lean. Vite + Bun delivers sub-second builds.

This isn't a graphing calculator. It's a computation framework with a rendering pipeline.

**[→ Repository](https://github.com/gouravgangwardev/maths-supernova)**

</details>
